Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

Abhyaasa (“the app”, “we”) is a music-practice tracker for children, used by parents, students, and teachers. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. Abhyaasa is designed to be used under a parent’s or guardian’s control.

Who controls a child’s information

A parent or guardian creates and owns each child’s profile. Nothing about a child is shared with anyone unless the parent chooses to invite a specific teacher. The parent can remove a teacher’s access or delete the child’s data at any time.

Information we collect

  • Account (only if you sign in): your email address and the display name you enter. Sign-in is optional — the Android app works fully offline without an account.
  • Child profile: the child’s first name (or nickname) and reward preferences that the parent enters.
  • Practice & class records: dates practiced, optional notes, and — if you use the fees feature — classes held, the fee set by the teacher, and monthly settlement records.
  • We do not collect location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for advertising. There are no ads and no third-party trackers in the app.

How we use it

Only to provide the app’s features: tracking practice, showing progress, letting a parent share progress with an invited teacher, and tracking classes and fees. We do not sell personal information or use children’s data for advertising or profiling.

Where it’s stored

When you sign in, data is stored in Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore) on Google Cloud infrastructure, protected by security rules so that only the owning parent, an invited teacher, and the operator can access a given child’s records. If you use the Android app without signing in, data stays on your device only.

Who can see a child’s data

  • The parent who owns the profile.
  • A teacher the parent explicitly invites — read-only, and only until the parent removes them.
  • The operator of the service — a single administrator who may access records across accounts strictly for support, safety, and abuse prevention. The operator never uses this access for advertising or profiling, and this access is protected by a dedicated, secured administrator account.

Your choices & deleting data

  • Remove a teacher at any time from the app.
  • Delete everything: use “Delete my child’s data” in the app to permanently remove the child’s profile, practice logs, classes, fees, teacher access, and your account sign-in.
  • You can also email us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data (see Contact).

Children’s privacy (COPPA & similar)

Abhyaasa is intended to be set up and managed by a parent or guardian. We collect only the limited information needed to run the app, use it only for that purpose, and give parents control to review and delete their child’s information. We do not knowingly collect personal information from a child without a parent’s involvement.

Security

Access is protected by authentication and server-side security rules. No system is perfectly secure, but we limit what is collected and who can access it.

Changes

We may update this policy; we’ll revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will be reflected here.
