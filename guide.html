Getting started

Two ways to use Abhyaasa — pick what suits you.

🌐 Web app Any device

Open abhyaasa-manthana.web.app on an iPhone, Android phone, or computer — nothing to install. On a phone, use your browser’s Add to Home Screen to open it like a real app. Teachers use the web app.

📱 Android app Offline + reminders

The Android app adds offline logging and a daily practice reminder. On the home page tap Download Android App, open Abhyaasa.apk, allow “Install unknown apps” if asked, then Install. It works fully offline; signing in adds cloud backup, teacher sharing, and fees.

For parents & students

Set up your child, log practice, and (optionally) share with a teacher.

1. Create your account & child profile

  1. Open the app and choose Parent / Student.
  2. Sign in with Google or an email & password. (Email sign-ups get a quick verification email — see Troubleshooting if it doesn’t arrive.)
  3. Enter your child’s name, pick a reward (⭐ star, 💲 dollar, 🎟️ sticker, or your own), and set a PIN that locks the Settings screen.

2. Log daily practice

  1. On the Practice calendar, tap today (or any past day).
  2. Mark practice done, optionally add a note your teacher can see, and save. Enjoy the celebration! 🎉
  3. Logged the wrong day or by mistake? Just tap that day again to undo or re-log.

Track progress under Earnings (totals) and Stars (streaks, badges, and a practice heatmap).

3. Daily reminder Android

In Settings, turn on Daily reminder and pick a time. You’ll get a gentle notification to practice. (Allow notifications when prompted.)

4. Add your child’s teacher (optional)

  1. Ask your teacher for their invite code (they generate it in the web app).
  2. In your app, open the teacher/sharing area and enter the code. Your teacher becomes a read-only viewer of practice and classes.
  3. You’re in control — you can remove the teacher anytime, which cuts off their access immediately.

5. Classes & fees

If your teacher tracks classes and a fee, you’ll see a monthly total and a Settle button. When you’ve paid, tap Settle to mark the month done. You’ll get a monthly settle reminder on the day & time your teacher chose.

6. Your data & privacy

You own your child’s data. In Settings → Delete my data you can permanently remove everything and your sign-in account. See the Privacy Policy for details.

🪕 Tanpura drone

A built-in practice drone to sing and play along with — on both the app and the web.

Where to find it

In the Android app, tap the 🪕 Tanpura tab along the bottom. In the web app, tap the round 🪕 button in the bottom-right corner — it’s on every screen. Tap the big ▶ to start the drone; tap it again to stop.

Set it up for your practice

  • Pitch (Sa) — choose your Sa with the – / + buttons, or tap the note to pick from the full list. Each note also shows the harmonium key (e.g. Mid · White 2), handy if you tune to a keyboard.
  • Fine tune — nudge the pitch a few cents sharp or flat to match another instrument exactly.
  • First string — set the top string to Pa, Ma, Ni, or Sa to suit the raga.
  • Speed & Volume — adjust how quickly the strings cycle and how loud it plays.

On Android the drone stops when you leave the Tanpura tab. On the web it keeps playing while you use the rest of the app, so you can practice as you go — reopen the panel and tap ■ to stop. It needs no internet and stores nothing.

For teachers

Use the web app to view students, track classes, and manage fees.

1. Sign in as a teacher

Open abhyaasa-manthana.web.app, choose Teacher, and sign in with Google or email.

2. Invite parents

  1. In your teacher area, find “Invite a parent” and copy your invite code (tap Generate the first time).
  2. Share the code with a parent. Once they enter it, their child appears in your student list — read-only.
  3. Need to reset it? New code makes a fresh one and the old code stops working.

3. Track classes & notes

For each student you can mark the days a class happened and add a short class note (what was taught) that the parent can see.

4. Fees & the monthly settle reminder

  1. Set a per-student fee (e.g. a per-class amount and currency).
  2. Choose the day & time each month the parent should be reminded to settle. Abhyaasa sends that reminder automatically.
  3. The billing overview shows each month’s classes, amount due, and whether the parent has settled.

Troubleshooting

Most issues have a quick fix — try these first.

I didn’t get the verification / password email
  1. Check your Spam / Junk and “Promotions” folders.
  2. Wait a minute — email can be slow — then use Resend.
  3. Double-check the address you typed has no typos.
  4. Still nothing? Try Google sign-in instead — it needs no email step.
I can’t sign in
  1. Make sure you’re online (Wi-Fi or data).
  2. Re-check your email & password; use Forgot password to reset.
  3. Use the same method you signed up with (Google vs. email).
The daily practice reminder isn’t showing Android
  1. In the app Settings, confirm Daily reminder is on and the time is right.
  2. In your phone’s Settings → Apps → Abhyaasa → Notifications, allow notifications.
  3. Turn off battery optimization for Abhyaasa (Settings → Apps → Abhyaasa → Battery → Unrestricted) so Android doesn’t delay the alarm.
  4. Open the app once after a phone restart so the reminder re-arms.
I didn’t get the monthly settle reminder
  1. Make sure you’ve signed in at least once and allowed notifications.
  2. The reminder only appears once your teacher has set a fee and a settle day/time.
  3. Same battery tip as above — keep Abhyaasa unrestricted.
My teacher can’t see my child (or updates)
  1. Confirm you entered the teacher’s invite code and the teacher shows as added.
  2. Both of you need to be online for changes to sync.
  3. Ask the teacher to refresh (reload the web page) — new logs appear within moments.
I entered the invite code but nothing happened
  1. Re-type the code carefully — no spaces, correct upper/lower case.
  2. Codes can be reset: ask the teacher for a New code and try again.
  3. Make sure you’re online when you submit it.
The web app looks out of date or stuck
  1. Refresh the page (pull down on mobile, or reload in your browser).
  2. Fully close and reopen the browser tab or the Home-Screen app.
  3. If it added to Home Screen a while ago, remove and re-add it to get the latest version.
I forgot my Settings PIN Android

The PIN only locks the Settings screen — your practice logging is unaffected. If you’re signed in (cloud backup on), clearing the app’s data or reinstalling resets the PIN and your data returns after you sign back in. If you were using the app offline with no account, clearing data also clears local logs — so set a fresh PIN you’ll remember.

A star shows on the wrong day

Each day’s star just means “practiced that day.” Tap the day to undo or re-log on the correct date. Your Earnings total counts every log, so logging more than once a day still adds up.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings → Delete my data. This permanently removes your child’s records and your sign-in account. It can’t be undone.
